# SubLim3 Nexus onboarding

## Printed Quick Start card

1. **Install the GM app.** Scan the GM App QR while the phone still has internet access.
2. **Power on Nexus.** Wait for the status light, then join the advertised Nexus Wi-Fi or select Home Wi-Fi during setup.
3. **Claim your Nexus.** Scan the unique device QR printed on the underside of the unit. Claiming replaces routine Admin and GM PIN entry; the PIN remains a recovery fallback during migration.
4. **Create your table.** Choose a game system, create a campaign, and add player characters.
5. **Invite players.** Open the campaign in the GM app and show its Player QR. Players scan it to open or install the Player app and join that campaign.
6. **Start playing.** Assign RFID cards, choose table audio, and publish the opening scene.

## QR responsibilities

The printed guide contains stable download links only:

- **GM App QR:** a universal link that opens the GM app store listing or installation page.
- **Player App QR:** an optional universal link that opens the Player app store listing or installation page.

The device and GM app generate scoped links:

- **Device Claim QR:** unique per Nexus unit and accepted only during a short physical pairing window.
- **Player Campaign QR:** generated by the GM app for one campaign. The current web migration links to `/player/?campaign={campaign_id}`; a later phase will replace it with a short-lived invitation code that survives native-app installation.

Static printed QR codes must never contain Admin credentials, device tokens, campaign secrets, or permanent Player authorization.

## Offline onboarding

Native apps must be installed before a phone leaves internet access for the Nexus hotspot. The guide should say this explicitly. A universal link should resume the claim or campaign-join flow after installation. The Nexus-hosted web GM and Player surfaces remain the offline fallback.

## Migration phases

1. Campaign-specific Player QR in the existing GM console.
2. Short-lived Player invitation service with GM approval and revocation.
3. Unique device claim secret and physical pairing window.
4. Native GM and Player app universal links and secure-keychain device credentials.
5. Retire routine PIN prompts while retaining local recovery.
